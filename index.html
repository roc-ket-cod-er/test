GAME 1: CAT DASH
A simple 3:2 game in which you use the q key to dash along with the WD keys for side to side movement and the space key to jump. Dashing to a side will result in you wrapping, effectively going from one side to the other.
Game 2: A platformer?
A 3:2 simple platformer. You can use the WASD keys or the arrow keys. Press the space key to dash. Your goal is the reach the red bar, from which you will be teleported to the next game.
Game 3: A Drawing
This is just a simple 16:9 drawing game. Click to draw. The color will change a bit only while you are clicking.
Game 4: A 3D Platformer
This 16:9 game, made by timcool, is a simple 3D platformer, in which you can use WASD to move, space key to jump, and click and drag to move the view point. Your goal is to reach the flag.
Game 5: SCRATCH CART!!!!
A simple 16:9 game in which you can use WASD keys or arows to race. Try to beat 90 seconds on the second track
